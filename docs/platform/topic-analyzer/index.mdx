Topic Analyzer provides automated recommendations for your Kafka topics, helping you to optimize your configurations.
Topic Analyzer will provide recommendations for settings such as linger.ms, batch.size, and compression. By implementing
the recommendations, you can enhance throughput and reduce latency, ultimately lowering the costs of running Kafka.

title: Self-service

<Check>This feature is available with [Conduktor Scale Plus](https://conduktor.io/scale) only.</Check> 

Conduktor's Self-service framework helps you scale Kafka usage in your organization by facilitating collaboration between central platform and application teams.

It centers around a GitOps approach to resource management, access requests, and brings governance into your enterprise through policy enforcement and strong ownership principles.

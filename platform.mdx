---
title: What is Conduktor Console?
---

Conduktor Console (previously Platform) is the centralized UI-based control plane for managing Kafka.

## Streamline Apache Kafka

Conduktor will transform your approach and interactions with Apache Kafka. We give you a single platform for Kafka management, testing, monitoring, data quality, security, and data governance.

Console is one of the main [Conduktor artifacts](/guide/conduktor-in-production/deploy-artifacts/index) and together with [Gateway](/guide/conduktor-in-production/deploy-artifacts/deploy-gateway/index) make up the Conduktor platform:
